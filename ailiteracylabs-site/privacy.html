Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 29, 2025

Welcome to AI Literacy Labs. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at [Your Contact Email Address].

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you interact with our services, including our website and our Chrome Extension.

Information from our Website

  • Email Address: We collect your email address when you sign up for our waitlist to send you communications about our product.
  • Voluntary Feedback: We collect any information you choose to provide in optional feedback fields to help us build a better product.

Information Collected via our Chrome Extension

To provide our bookmarking service, the Extension collects the following information:

  • Personally Identifiable Information: When you sign in using Google, we collect your **email address** to identify your account and associate saved content with your profile.
  • Authentication Information: We securely store **authentication tokens** in your browser's local storage to keep you logged in to your AI Literacy Labs account.
  • Web History & Website Content: When you choose to save a page, we collect the **URL, title, and metadata** (such as description text and thumbnail images) of that page. This is collected only upon your explicit action to save the content.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send you communications. We may use your email address to send you information about product launches, beta access, and occasional feedback requests.
  • To provide our service. Data collected via the Chrome Extension is used to provide the core functionality of bookmarking web content to your personal account.
  • To authenticate you. We use your Google account information and stored tokens to securely manage your session.
  • To improve our product. We analyze feedback and usage patterns to guide our product development.

3. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties for their promotional purposes. Your information is stored securely with our trusted service providers who help us operate our services, including **Supabase** for our backend database and authentication, and others for form capture and email communications. All providers are committed to high standards of data protection.

4. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We aim to protect your personal information through a system of organizational and technical security measures. We have implemented measures designed to secure your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure.

5. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

Based on the laws of some countries, you may have the right to request access to the personal information we collect from you, change that information, or delete it. You can manage your saved content directly within your AI Literacy Labs dashboard. You can also unsubscribe from our marketing email list at any time by clicking on the unsubscribe link in the emails that we send or by contacting us.

6. Updates To This Notice

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this privacy notice frequently to be informed of how we are protecting your information.
